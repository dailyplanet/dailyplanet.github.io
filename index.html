    FORMING THE CLUSTER - THE BIG BIRTH

  • Place the white ball as shown.

  • Place the balls numbered 1 (yellow), 2 (blue) and 3 (red) on the table as shown. These three balls together are known as the Cluster.

  • Once the Cluster has been birthed, you are ready to bang.

  • Oh and be sure to have some drinks ready, you filthy animal.

    HOW TO PLAY

  • The aim of the game is to sink the Cluster balls (yellow, red, and blue) into the Black Holes (pockets). Sinking the yellow ball awards your opponent with a drink, sinking the blue ball awards them two drinks, and sinking the red awards them three.

  • Once a Cluster ball is sunk, it is to be placed back on the table on the D dots from left to right as shown

  • You may sink the Cluster balls in any order.

  • If you fail to sink a Cluster ball, your turn it over and it is the next players turn.

  • If you succeed in sinking a Cluster ball, your opponent drinks and you get another turn.

    • ORIONS BELTS

  • Once all three dots on the D are occupied, this formes a belt. Orion's belt. What does this mean? It depends on the Cluster colour order.

  • Orion's Belt: Yellow (1), Blue (2), Red (3) from left to right. This combination WINS YOU THE GAME.

  • Orion's Evening Belt: Any other order. This unordered combination means your opponents must use their opposite hand to play, until they are able to reverse this by getting an Orion's Evening Belt of their own. Note: You may still win the game whilst wearing Orion's Evening Belt.
